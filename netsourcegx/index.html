NetsourceGX

NetsourceGX is a Customized Application as a Service (CAaaS) for public sector clients.

Lets us take away your headaches of security, software development, hosting and on-going operations and maintenance.

Security

Our company is focused on a “Security First” approach. Public sector applications require stringent security requirements. We leverage Amazon’s cloud-based infrastructure. This will allow you to inherit many of the controls of a Fedramp compliant infrastructure. We use the shared responsibility model and will help you comply with your various security and compliance requirements.

Application Development

Think of us as an extension of your team. You do not need to hire software developers, user experience specialists, testers, or project managers. We will provide a team that will support your needs that is flexible and has built public sector applications in a rapid manner using our framework, solution kits and reusable components.

Hosting

We use a secured and hardened Amazon Web Hosting layer that is monitored and scalable to meet your various needs. (Microsoft Azure offering in development). Each client has their own separate instances that is dedicated for their use vs a shared tenant model.

Operations and Maintenance

We will build your custom applications and provide on-going operations and maintenance. We can respond to your needs if you need to have additional enhancements or major projects in a quick and agile manner.
